It offers a curated selection of videos that explore the mindset needed for modern entrepreneurship. The toolbox highlights emotional intelligence, ethical leadership, adaptability, and inclusion—skills often overlooked in traditional business education. It encourages reflection, discussion, and integration into both teaching and independent learning.
outcomes: Users develop a broader understanding of entrepreneurship that goes beyond finance and business planning. The toolbox raises awareness of key human-centred competencies such as emotional intelligence, ethics, adaptability, and inclusion. Learners become more reflective and better prepared to navigate real-world challenges with a responsible and resilient mindset. The videos also support more engaging and relatable entrepreneurship education, helping educators introduce complex topics through accessible content.
