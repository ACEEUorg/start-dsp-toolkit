name: Venture Ethically
summary: Venture Ethically is a project-based toolbox for educators and student innovation hubs to engage learners in adapting ethical, inclusive business ideas to real-world community needs through structured design thinking and peer reflection.
description: Venture Ethically is a flexible project-based toolbox that enables students to adapt youth-friendly business ideas to specific community challenges using a structured ethical innovation process. Participants choose ventures from a curated list, apply local constraints, define their give-back strategy, and complete worksheets and canvas activities. They then pitch their adapted idea and receive feedback from peers and educators.
outcomes: |
  Understanding of ethical entrepreneurship and inclusive innovation
  Practical application of social impact principles
  Peer-to-peer evaluation and reflective learning
  Strengthening of EntreComp and GreenComp dimensions
  Awareness of community engagement and give-back strategies
instructions: |
  1. Students are assigned a start-up and a community profile.
  2. Using the workbook and canvas, they adapt the venture ethically and inclusively.
  3. They prepare and deliver a pitch.
  4. Peer and teacher feedback is collected using the assessment sheets.
  5. Educators support the process via the Digital Project Guide.
