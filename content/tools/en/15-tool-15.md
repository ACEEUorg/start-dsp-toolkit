name: Sustainable Business Model Canvas
summary: The Sustainable Business Model Canvas helps university educators integrate sustainability into business model teaching. It enables students to design business models that combine economic, social, and environmental value.
description: The Sustainable Business Model Canvas Guideline is designed for educators in entrepreneurship, business, and sustainability. It supports the integration of sustainability principles into teaching by providing a structured method for analyzing and developing business models that generate economic, environmental, and social value. The tool helps students understand the broader impact of entrepreneurial decisions and equips them with practical skills to design responsible, future-oriented ventures.
outcomes: |
  Students design business models that integrate environmental and social dimensions.
  Learners develop a systems-thinking approach to entrepreneurship.
  Increased awareness of sustainability trade-offs in business planning.
  Improved ability to critically assess long-term business impact.
  Enhanced teamwork and communication skills through collaborative modeling.
  Educators gain a structured method for teaching sustainable entrepreneurship.
  Universities foster a culture of responsible innovation.
instructions: |
  Step 1: Preparation
  •	Introduce the Business Model Canvas as a base framework.
  •	Present the SBMC and explain its extended purpose.
  •	Discuss sustainability dimensions and the relevance for business strategy.
  •	Distribute blank SBMC templates (physical or digital).
  Step 2: Business Model Development
  •	Ask students to develop or refine a business idea using the SBMC.
  •	Use guiding questions for each building block.
  •	Work in teams to complete the canvas collaboratively.
  Step 3: Presentation & Feedback
  •	Each team presents their SBMC to peers or mentors.
  •	Encourage reflection on the feasibility and impact of the business model.
  •	Provide structured feedback using sustainability and viability criteria.
